Brief Introduction into This Jesus' Family

The Omidiji family is a christian family of three boys and three girls. Gender balance you may want to say, you can say that again and again.

Could you believe none of the family members ever bother to ask if they actually have a mother? Interesting!

Our father has always been telling us from childhood that we have no mother, but it remains a mystery. In all fairness to our dad, we never for a day feel the absence of our mum. In fact, our curiosity to ask for our mum was because we hear that word 'mum' in school from our colleagues...
